Our Story

Mama Mac’s isn’t just a restaurant; it’s an extension of our family dining room. We opened our doors in Ashland because we believe there is nothing quite like the power of a hot, home-cooked meal to bring people together.

Whether you've lived in Alabama your whole life or you're just passing through on the highway, we promise to serve you real food, made with real love. Pull up a chair—supper's ready.
